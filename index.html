🛕 Divine Detour

Temples Nearby

Since you're travelling to this sacred region, don't miss these beautiful temples just a short distance away. A perfect spiritual experience for everyone!

🏛️
Sri Lakshmi Narasimha Swamy Temple
✦ Yadadri-Bhuvangiri ✦
📍 ~5 km from Bondhugula · On your route!
One of Telangana's most revered pilgrimage sites, recently renovated with grand gopurams and intricate carvings. Lord Narasimha manifested here in five forms — a breathtakingly beautiful must-visit!
🗺️ View on Maps
🕌
Surendrapuri — Kashi Vishwanath
✦ Surendrapuri ✦
📍 ~20 km · Near Yadadri
A unique spiritual park recreating the sacred atmosphere of Kashi. Features 108 Shiva Lingas, divine sculptures and the famous 85-ft Kailash Mountain — extraordinary for devotees of all ages.
🗺️ View on Maps
⛰️
Swarnagiri (Golden Hill Temple)
✦ Rajapet · Yadadri District ✦
📍 ~10 km · En route to venue
Nestled atop a scenic golden hill near Rajapet with panoramic Deccan views. The trek is a refreshing spiritual experience with deep local significance — very close to Bondhugula village!
🗺️ View on Maps
